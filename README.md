# diff-geo-directed-study

Notes, syllabus, and study materials for an independent study in Differential Geometry.

This repository contains my independent directed study notes, curriculum, and materials on Differential Geometry.

## 📚 Core Curriculum & Textbooks

The trajectory of this study synthesizes classical surface theory with modern differential-geometric methods in mathematical physics:

1. **Primary Text:** *Differential Geometry of Curves and Surfaces* by Manfredo P. do Carmo.
2. **Supplementary Text:** *Elementary Differential Geometry* by Barrett O'Neill.
3. **Advanced References:** John M. Lee’s *Introduction to Smooth Manifolds* and *Introduction to Riemannian Manifolds*, Theodore Frankel’s *The Geometry of Physics*, and Robert Wald’s *General Relativity*.

## 🎯 Core Objectives

* **Formalize Derivations:** Reconstruct major geometric derivations step-by-step, including curvature formulas, geodesic equations, and tensor computations.
* **Mathematical Continuity:** Connect classical surface geometry with smooth manifolds, Riemannian geometry, and Lorentzian geometry.
* **Expository Writing:** Author clean, LaTeX-compiled research notes detailing curvature tensors, covariant derivatives, geodesics, and geometric formulations of gauge theory and general relativity.
